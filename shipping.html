Shipping Information

Welcome to Yakuzaboss! We are delighted to offer unique anime-inspired clothing to fans around the globe. Below you will find all the information needed regarding our shipping policies.

Shipping Destinations

We ship worldwide, so you can enjoy our products no matter where you are located.

Shipping Rates

Domestic:

Standard shipping is free for orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat rate of $5 is charged.

International:

A flat rate of $15 applies to all international orders. Please note that you are responsible for any customs duties or taxes imposed by your country.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been shipped.

Delivery Times

Domestic: Please allow 3-5 business days for delivery.

International: Delivery can take 7-21 business days depending on your location and local customs processing times.
